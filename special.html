Important Dates To Know!


Transgender Day of Visibility (TDOV)

Observed on March 31st each year, TDOV celebrates the accomplishments and contributions of transgender people worldwide while raising awareness of the discrimination they face.


Transgender Day of Remembrance (TDoR)

Held annually on November 20th, TDoR memorializes transgender individuals who have been murdered as a result of transphobia and draws attention to the violence and prejudice faced by the transgender community.


International Transgender Day of Visibility (ITDoV)

A companion day to TDOV, ITDoV on March 31st focuses on celebrating and empowering transgender and gender non-conforming people globally.


Spirit Day

Observed on the third Thursday in October, Spirit Day is a day of action to stand against bullying of LGBTQ+ youth, including those who are transgender..


National Coming Out Day

Celebrated on October 11th, this day encourages LGBTQ+ individuals to come out and share their stories to promote visibility and acceptance.


Pride Month (June)

While not exclusively for transgender individuals, Pride Month includes events and celebrations that highlight the diversity and achievements of LGBTQ+ communities, including transgender people.


Transgender Awareness Week

Occurring annually during the second week of November, this week includes events and educational activities to raise awareness about issues facing transgender people.


Transgender Day of Action

Held in late June, this day emphasizes direct action and activism in support of transgender rights and visibility.
