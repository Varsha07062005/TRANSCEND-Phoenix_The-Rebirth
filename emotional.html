IF NOT YOU THEN WHO?!

We plan to create an online portal specifically focusing on the transgender community. Our goal is to provide educational resources starting from elementary education and continuing through advanced learning, ensuring they are supported throughout their educational journey. Additionally, we will integrate online work-from-home opportunities to provide employment options. We aim to support this community by interviewing them to understand their basic requirements and the difficulties they face. We will also motivate them by featuring well recognized and appreciated transgender individuals, thereby creating awareness and fostering social acceptance.
